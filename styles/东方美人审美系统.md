# 东方美人审美系统

Use this reference whenever the user asks for 东方美人, 东方审美, 美人风格, 气质, 柔美, 妩媚, 成熟, 优雅, 高贵, 清冷, 温婉, 英气, 神性, 慵懒, or wants a stronger aesthetic direction before choosing a concrete scene route.

This file is an upper-level aesthetic layer. It does not replace route files such as `东方幻想古风.md`, `古典东方美人.md`, `现代东方美人.md`, or `甜系纯欲生活写真.md`; it gives them a human beauty direction.

## Core Rule

东方美人 is not only clothing, background, or historical symbols.

A strong image should make beauty visible through:

- gaze
- posture
- hand gesture
- shoulder-neck line
- hair rhythm
- fabric behavior
- light on skin and jewelry
- emotional restraint
- spatial identity

Do not let spectacle erase the woman. The route, props, and environment must frame the subject, not replace her.

## Director Selection

When a user gives only a broad request:

- `东方美人` defaults to `优雅 + 柔美`.
- `古风美人` defaults to `古典优雅 + 温婉`, unless the user asks for fantasy spectacle.
- `妩媚` should become `mature charming allure`, not exposure.
- `高贵` should become ritual identity, posture, and composition, not just expensive ornaments.
- `成熟` should become controlled gaze, composed posture, deep palette, and material weight.
- `清冷` should still include emotion in the eyes; avoid mannequin-like blankness.

## Body And Gesture Language

Use visible, filmable gestures:

- turning back while holding a curtain
- touching an earring
- adjusting a jade button
- lifting a teacup but pausing before drinking
- holding a book open with one finger
- resting one hand on a carved screen
- brushing hair behind the ear
- holding a lantern low enough to light the face
- standing at a window with shoulders relaxed
- stepping through a doorway as fabric trails behind

Avoid:

- idle hands
- stiff standing
- exaggerated S-curve posing
- broken-joint flexibility
- camera angles that turn the body into a fetish object

## Face Direction

Use face language to create difference between beauties:

- soft oval face, gentle eyes, low saturation lip color for 柔美 / 温婉
- calm almond eyes, thin eyeliner, restrained red lips for 优雅 / 成熟
- phoenix eyes, high crown, direct gaze for 高贵 / 神性
- clear brows, tied hair, sharper gaze for 英气
- slightly lowered eyelids, loose hair, warm skin light for 慵懒 / 妩媚

Do not over-lock one face. Keep an aesthetic family, not a clone.

## Material And Light

Beauty should be tactile:

- silk should have weight, folds, and edge highlights
- gauze should be an outer layer, not a revealing device
- jade should be cool and quiet
- gold should be controlled, not sprayed everywhere
- pearls should soften the face and neck
- candlelight should shape eyes, hands, and jewelry
- rain light should flatten noise and make skin natural
- moonlight should cut a clean rim, not wash out the face

## Combination Examples

- `绮罗金帐 + 妩媚 + 高贵`: red-gold palace chamber, candle haze, jewel chains, dignified gaze.
- `宋韵茶影 + 温婉 + 优雅`: tea room, low table, pale silk, hand on cup, quiet rain.
- `洛神水镜 + 柔美 + 神性`: water mirror, floating sleeves, pearl-white light, soft direct gaze.
- `青瓷月影 + 清冷 + 高贵`: porcelain palette, moonlit screen, jade ornaments, still posture.
- `旗袍电影感 + 成熟 + 妩媚`: old cinema light, qipao-inspired tailoring, hand near earring, restrained red lip.
- `东方静奢 + 成熟 + 优雅`: black silk, jade accent, warm wood, composed gaze, minimal set.

## Safety And Taste

- Keep the subject adult and clearly mature when the prompt includes 妩媚, 私房, 浴后, 寝殿, or 纯欲.
- Build attraction through gaze, posture, fabric, light, and atmosphere.
- Avoid nudity, transparent revealing fabric, lingerie styling, voyeuristic framing, foot-focused framing, and age-ambiguous language.
