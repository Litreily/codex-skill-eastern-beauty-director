## Prompt Assembly Rule

When assembling a prompt:

1. Start from relationship and story.
2. Define the two women as distinct adult East Asian identities.
3. Choose scene, season, weather, time, and light from the libraries.
4. Choose material and costume from the season and scene.
5. Choose lens and framing based on interaction distance.
6. Add material separation and body-boundary rules.
7. Add negative prompt.

## Example

Input:

```text
Skill: AncientFemaleCompanionship
Scene: LakePavilion
Season: Summer
Weather: LightRain
Time: Sunset
Story: UmbrellaSharing
Emotion: Dependence
Costume: Jiangnan
Lens: 85mm
```

Expected narrative:

Two close companions walking through a Jiangnan lakeside pavilion during light summer rain. One quietly tilts the umbrella toward the other, allowing her own shoulder to become wet. The younger notices and gently reaches for her sleeve. Warm sunset reflections shimmer across the water.
