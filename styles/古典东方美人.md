# 古典东方美人

Use this reference when the user asks for 古典东方美人, 古典美人, 宋韵, 唐宫, 江南, 洛神, 青瓷, 昆曲, 闺阁, 仕女, 东方古典优雅, or a non-fantasy classical Eastern beauty image. If the user asks for mythic spectacle, load `东方幻想古风.md` instead or use this file as a classical accent.

## Core Rule

Classical Eastern beauty is built through restraint, line, material, gesture, and atmosphere. It can be soft, charming, mature, noble, poetic, or sacred without becoming generic hanfu photography.

Use `styles/东方美人审美系统.md` first when the user asks for a beauty temperament such as 柔美, 妩媚, 成熟, 优雅, 高贵, 清冷, 温婉, 英气, 神性, or 慵懒.

## Photography Language

- Lens: 85mm portrait lens by default; use 50mm for a fuller environmental story.
- Camera height: eye-level or slight low angle; avoid voyeuristic high angle and distorted ultra-wide angle.
- Framing: face and eyes first, hand gesture second, fabric and setting third.
- Light: soft window light, candlelight, moonlight, tea steam highlight, rain bounce, or lantern glow.
- Depth: shallow to medium depth of field; background remains readable but never louder than the face.
- Texture: real silk, matte linen, porcelain, wood, paper window, rain reflection, candle smoke.

## Route Notes

### luoshen-water-mirror

- Temperament: 柔美 + 神性.
- Role: river goddess, water-mirror beauty, pearl-sleeve apparition.
- Costume: pearl-white layered silk, pale blue-green pibo, subtle shell or pearl ornaments, opaque inner robe.
- Scene: shallow water mirror, river mist, lotus leaves, moon reflection, silk sleeves touching water.
- Gesture: one hand lifting wet sleeve, one hand touching water surface, soft direct gaze.
- Light: pearl moonlight, soft water bounce, silver rim on hair.
- Avoid: mermaid styling, swimsuit cues, transparent revealing fabric.

### song-tea-shadow

- Temperament: 温婉 + 优雅.
- Role: Song-style tea beauty, quiet scholar lady, incense-room hostess.
- Costume: pale ivory or warm gray cross-collar robe, narrow clean lines, jade button, matte silk.
- Scene: low tea table, wood lattice window, white wall, ceramic cup, incense thread, rain or morning light.
- Gesture: lifting a teacup but pausing, touching a tea whisk, turning slightly from the window.
- Light: soft window light, tea steam highlight, calm shadow.
- Avoid: overloaded ornaments, fantasy magic, loud color.

### tang-palace-night-banquet

- Temperament: 成熟 + 妩媚 + 高贵.
- Role: Tang palace musician, banquet beauty, flower-crown noblewoman.
- Costume: Tang-inspired layered robe, structured bodice over opaque layers, flower crown, gold hairpins, embroidered shawl.
- Scene: palace banquet, carved screen, candle rows, lacquer tray, musical instrument, mural-like background.
- Gesture: pausing with pipa, turning from a banquet screen, one hand adjusting a hairpin.
- Light: warm amber candlelight, deep red and antique gold shadows.
- Avoid: belly-dance costume, excessive exposure, drunken party mood.

### jiangnan-rain-beauty

- Temperament: 温婉 + 柔美.
- Role: rain-window beauty, Jiangnan traveler, riverside inn lady.
- Costume: pale blue, ivory, ink-gray or moss-green robe, soft fabric, simple hairpin.
- Scene: white walls and black tiles, rain-streaked window, stone bridge, umbrella, wet wood railing.
- Gesture: holding umbrella edge, touching rain glass, looking back from a covered corridor.
- Light: rainy gray bounce light, small warm lamp, wet reflection.
- Avoid: tourist costume feeling, subject too small in scenery.

### celadon-moon-shadow

- Temperament: 清冷 + 高贵.
- Role: celadon moon lady, porcelain chamber oracle, jade-screen noblewoman.
- Costume: celadon, moon-white, pale jade silk, cool pearl ornaments, clean collar.
- Scene: moonlit screen, porcelain vase, jade table, blue-gray wall, single branch.
- Gesture: standing still with one hand on a porcelain vase, touching a jade pendant, looking past the camera.
- Light: cool moon rim light, porcelain reflection, minimal warm fill.
- Avoid: lifeless blank face, excessive blue filter.

### kunqu-flower-shadow

- Temperament: 妩媚 + 优雅.
- Role: Kunqu-inspired beauty, flower-window performer, opera-hand muse.
- Costume: opera-inspired collar or sleeve logic, embroidered silk, hair ornament, but not full stage costume unless requested.
- Scene: flower lattice window, painted folding screen, dim candle, garden shadow.
- Gesture: orchid hand, sleeve covering half a smile, turning eyes before turning body.
- Light: flower-shadow pattern, warm side light, soft face highlight.
- Avoid: heavy stage makeup, exaggerated theatrical grin.

### bamboo-literati-beauty

- Temperament: 成熟 + 温婉 + 知性.
- Role: literati beauty, chess companion, book-room noblewoman.
- Costume: quiet silk or linen robe, ink-gray, ivory, bamboo green accents, small jade hairpin.
- Scene: bamboo grove study, book table, ink stone, go board, tea vessel, long window.
- Gesture: holding a book open, placing a chess piece, brushing sleeve away from ink.
- Light: bamboo shadow, late afternoon side light, paper-window softness.
- Avoid: academic costume, modern office cues.

### rouge-snow

- Temperament: 清冷 + 成熟 + 冷艳.
- Role: red-robed snow beauty, lantern oath keeper, winter palace visitor.
- Costume: deep red cloak or robe, black hair, white fur-like trim only if tasteful, gold or jade hairpin.
- Scene: snow courtyard, red lantern, black tile roof, cold stone steps, breath mist.
- Gesture: holding lantern close to face, looking back in falling snow, one hand gathering cloak.
- Light: lantern warm face light, cold snow rim, high contrast red-white-black.
- Avoid: Christmas palette, fantasy armor overload.

### jade-chamber-spring

- Temperament: 慵懒 + 柔美.
- Role: spring chamber beauty, poetry reader, flower-shadow lady.
- Costume: soft opaque silk robe, pale pink, ivory, warm jade, loose but covered layers.
- Scene: daybed or low couch, spring curtain, flower branch, book, afternoon window light.
- Gesture: leaning on an elbow while reading, looking up after being called, letting a sleeve fall naturally.
- Light: warm afternoon light, curtain diffusion, flower shadow on fabric.
- Avoid: bed-centered erotic framing, exposed chest, lingerie, voyeuristic angle.

## Prompt Assembly

1. Choose one route and one dominant aesthetic from `东方美人审美系统.md`.
2. Define the woman through gaze, posture, hand gesture, hair, and fabric before describing the background.
3. Use one story moment: pausing, turning, lifting, touching, listening, reading, or stepping through.
4. Let the classical setting support identity and mood.
5. Keep clothing opaque and tasteful; use gauze as atmosphere, not exposure.
