# Gufeng Visual Library

## Subgenres

- Eastern fantasy key visual: mythic role, iconic artifact, wonder-scale environment, supernatural phenomenon, strict color system.
- Jiangnan poetic: water town, rain, paper umbrella, white walls, black tiles, willow, mist.
- Palace elegance: corridors, lacquered screens, lanterns, restrained posture, silk and gold thread.
- Wuxia: bamboo forest, cliff path, sword, wind, sharp silhouette, decisive gaze.
- Xianxia: clouds, moonlight, floating silk, jade, glowing talismans, ethereal but controlled.
- Tang-inspired: high-waisted ruqun, full sleeves, peony, warm gold, confident expression.
- Song-inspired: narrow sleeves, restrained palette, scholar garden, refined minimalism.
- Dunhuang-inspired: murals, flying ribbons, mineral pigments, desert light, ornate jewelry.
- Snow mountain: fur-trimmed cloak, cold rim light, blue-white palette, breath mist.
- Night banquet: candles, bronze mirror, pipa, amber light, layered shadows.

## Face, Hair, Makeup

- Face: oval face, calm eyes, soft cheek contour, natural skin texture, subtle asymmetry.
- Expression: lowered gaze, slight smile, composed sorrow, alert stillness, gentle resolve.
- Hair: high bun, low bun, cloud-like updo, loose side strands, wind-touched hair.
- Hair ornaments: jade hairpin, pearl tassels, gold buyao, silver comb, floral pin.
- Makeup: soft rouge, thin eyeliner, bitten-lip tint, huadian, pearl powder glow.

## Clothing

- Silhouettes: ruqun, beizi, pibo, daxiushan, aoqun, cloak, wide sleeves.
- Fabrics: silk gauze, brocade, satin, raw silk, embroidered organza, translucent outer robe over opaque inner layers.
- Motifs: crane, lotus, cloud, bamboo, peony, wave, mountain, auspicious knot.
- Details: layered collar, embroidered cuffs, waist sash, jade pendant, flowing ribbon.

## Settings and Props

- Settings: waterside pavilion, bamboo forest, palace corridor, mountain temple, snow bridge, moonlit courtyard, lotus pond, old library, painted screen, desert grotto.
- Props: oil-paper umbrella, folding fan, pipa, guqin, sword, lantern, scroll, porcelain cup, jade pendant, bronze mirror, flower branch.
- Wonder settings: floating observatory, cloud sea palace, underwater dragon court, moon palace alchemy hall, bronze oracle city, giant lotus dream lake, suspended Kunlun bridge, glowing Dunhuang grotto.
- Iconic artifacts: jade astrolabe, bronze armillary sphere, phoenix lantern, cloud sword, tide pearl, talisman wheel, moon mirror, living mural ribbon, oracle bone tablet.

## Lighting and Color

- Light: soft window light, candlelight, moon rim light, rain-reflected light, dawn mist, lantern backlight, snow bounce light.
- Palettes: ink black and ivory, jade green and pearl white, cinnabar and gold, mist blue and silver, plum red and dark teal, sand ochre and mineral blue.

## Camera Language

- Portrait: 85mm lens, medium close-up, shallow depth of field, eye-level intimacy.
- Cinematic: 50mm lens, three-quarter shot, layered foreground, leading lines.
- Epic: low angle, wide shot, clouds or architecture framing the figure.
- Key visual: full-body or three-quarter figure, strong silhouette, halo-like artifact behind the subject, foreground motion, clear environmental scale.
- Detail: close-up on sleeve embroidery, hand holding jade pendant, raindrops on umbrella edge.
