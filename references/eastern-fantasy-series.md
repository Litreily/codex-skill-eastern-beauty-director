# Eastern Fantasy Series Library

Use this reference when the user wants "东方幻想", a stronger visual hook, a character lineup, a series, a game key visual, or says the result is generic.

## Series Design Rule

Every series entry needs:

- Route: one primary route from `styles/东方幻想古风.md`.
- Role: a readable fantasy profession or destiny.
- Symbol: one iconic prop or supernatural motif.
- Territory: a place that implies a world beyond the portrait.
- Color system: 2-4 controlled colors.
- Motion: fabric, light, water, clouds, sand, talismans, or weapon trails.
- Conflict: what is at stake in the image.

Avoid "pretty person standing in a scene." Make the image a frozen story moment.

## Strong Role Archetypes

- Celestial cartographer-priestess: jade astrolabe, bronze armillary sphere, dragon-shaped star map, floating observatory, deep teal + antique gold.
- Kunlun sword guardian: ice jade sword, snow bridge, mountain temple, silver breath mist, moon silver + ink black.
- Dunhuang mural oracle: mineral pigments, flying ribbons, desert grotto, luminous wall paintings, cinnabar + mineral blue + sand gold.
- Tide dragon envoy: tide pearl, coral crown, underwater palace, dragon shadows in waves, tide blue + pearl white + coral red.
- Lotus dream exorcist: lotus lantern, dream lake, paper talismans, mirror water, jade green + pale pink + ink.
- Phoenix lantern queen: palace fire court, phoenix lanterns, lacquer screens, ember ribbons, cinnabar + black lacquer + gold.
- Moon palace alchemist: moon mirror, silver furnace, osmanthus branches, floating elixir bowls, moon silver + warm amber.
- Bronze city war seer: oracle bones, bronze mask, cracked city gate, storm clouds, verdigris + dark bronze + lightning white.

## Spectacle Patterns

- Halo object: astrolabe, moon mirror, talisman wheel, dragon gate, mural circle behind the character.
- Environmental scale: tiny figure against enormous bronze sphere, cloud palace, giant moon, dragon shadow, mountain gate.
- Elemental trails: star ribbons, ink clouds, tide arcs, sword snow, phoenix sparks, lotus light.
- Layered depth: foreground ribbons or talismans, midground character, background wonder architecture.
- Story tension: opening a forbidden star map, guarding a sealed bridge, summoning a tide dragon, stepping out of a mural.

## Prompt Upgrade Moves

If a generated concept feels ordinary:

1. Replace "beauty" with a role and duty.
2. Replace "background" with a territory and architecture.
3. Replace "decorative prop" with a plot-driving artifact.
4. Replace "nice lighting" with a motivated supernatural light source.
5. Replace "portrait" with key visual composition: strong silhouette, halo object, foreground motion, clear scale.
