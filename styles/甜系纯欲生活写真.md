Composition is not the same as aspect ratio; `9:16` is only a canvas.

Recommended composition choices:

- Upper-body close-up: best for romantic feeling, eye contact, and pure-desire.
- Thigh-up framing: default recommendation. Best balance of face, bust-waist curve, thighs, and action.
- Full body: good for seaside, balcony, park, and walking scenes.
- Face close-up: good for emotional expression.

Avoid:

- environmental portrait where the person is too small
- background stealing attention
- excessive empty scene

When writing a 甜系纯欲生活写真 prompt:

1. Start with realism, feminine charm, romantic feeling, story moment, and natural pure-desire.
2. Immediately apply the body system hard lock.
3. Build the story moment before describing pose or expression.
