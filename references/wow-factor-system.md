# Wow Factor System

Use this reference when the image needs to be more eye-catching, less bland, more scroll-stopping, or more suitable for a series cover. The goal is not adding more decoration; the goal is one unmistakable first-glance hook.

## First-Glance Rule

Every strong prompt needs one dominant wow device:

- Giant graphic shape: huge moon, armillary sphere, dragon gate, phoenix wing, circular talisman wheel, vortex tide.
- Impossible transformation: mural becoming alive, robe turning into clouds, sword splitting snow into constellations, lantern unfolding a ghost city.
- Scale contrast: small heroine against enormous palace, dragon shadow, mountain gate, bronze mechanism, sea vortex.
- Dangerous action: standing on a breaking bridge, opening a forbidden seal, walking out of a burning palace, facing a dragon tide.
- Unusual viewpoint: top-down over a circular ritual platform, low angle against a giant halo object, foreground artifact framing the face.
- Light event: one artifact creates the main light, not generic ambient glow.

Choose one primary device and make everything else support it.

## Anti-Bland Upgrade Formula

If a prompt feels ordinary, apply this sequence:

1. Replace background with a spectacle event: "grotto wall opens into living stars", "cloud observatory tilts above a storm", "dragon tide coils around palace gate".
2. Replace prop with a plot device: "astrolabe opens forbidden map", "lotus lantern reveals a ghost city", "sword locks a collapsing bridge".
3. Replace static pose with action under tension: reaching, sealing, summoning, cutting, stepping through, holding a line.
4. Add a dominant silhouette: circular halo, diagonal sword slash, S-shaped ribbon, spiral tide, phoenix wing, giant gate.
5. Tighten color to one signature palette with one accent.

## Route-Specific Wow Devices

### celestial-empire

- Giant bronze armillary sphere cracks open like a celestial gate.
- Jade astrolabe projects a dragon-shaped star map wrapping behind the heroine.
- Top-down ritual platform with star lines converging under her feet.
- Cloud observatory tilts over a storm sea of clouds.

### kunlun-snow-sword

- Ice jade sword cuts the snowstorm into a clean crescent of moonlight.
- Suspended bridge breaks behind her while she holds the seal.
- Giant mountain gate frozen mid-collapse.
- Sword aura forms a pale dragon spine across the sky.

### dunhuang-mural-oracle

- The fresco wall tears open as painted celestial figures become real light.
- A huge mural halo frames the heroine while ribbons form an S-curve across the image.
- The floor becomes sand, the wall becomes sky, and she steps between both worlds.
- Mineral pigment streams flow from her sleeves like liquid color.

### tide-dragon-court

- Tide pearl opens a spiral vortex with a dragon silhouette inside.
- Underwater palace gate is split by moonlight from the surface.
- Her robe trails become translucent wave fins while keeping hanfu silhouette.
- A giant dragon eye appears behind the gate, partly hidden by water.

### lotus-dream-exorcist

- Lotus lantern reveals an inverted ghost city under the lake surface.
- Paper talismans form a circular seal around the moon reflection.
- She stands on one blooming lotus while the lake cracks into dream fragments.
- A gentle ghost hand reaches from reflection, stopped by lantern light.

### phoenix-palace-fire

- Ember ribbons form one enormous phoenix wing behind her.
- Palace screens burn away into a golden oath circle.
- Phoenix lantern opens like a small sun, lighting only her face and hands.
- She stands still while a fire court collapses into controlled sparks around her.

### red-gold-luo-curtain

- A giant painted screen behind her opens into a red-gold palace dream.
- Hanging jewel chains and incense smoke form a circular halo around her face.
- Red silk curtains create an S-shaped frame across the foreground and background.
- A bronze candle stand lights only her gaze, hands, jewelry, and layered silk.

### moon-palace-alchemy

- A giant moon mirror opens into a second lunar palace behind her.
- Silver elixir bowls float upward against gravity around the furnace.
- Osmanthus branches grow through the moonlight and become luminous gold veins.
- A cold lunar halo and warm furnace glow split the composition into two worlds.

### bronze-oracle-city

- Oracle bones rise into a giant bronze mask halo behind her.
- Lightning reveals an army-shaped shadow beyond a cracked city gate.
- Bronze bells swing without wind while engraved prophecy lines ignite.
- A broken ritual platform forms a dark circular stage under her feet.

### mist-bath-boudoir

- Moonlit lattice reflection forms a circular halo behind her damp hair.
- Steam curls into pale lotus shapes around the face, hands, and silk sleeves.
- A candle flame in the foreground creates a soft golden veil across the lens.
- A water reflection shows her gaze differently from her real face, adding private tension.

## Composition Rules

- Use one dominant graphic path: circle, diagonal slash, spiral, S-curve, vertical gate, or wing shape.
- Keep the face readable. The wow device should frame the heroine, not swallow her.
- Avoid equal-weight decoration everywhere. One big thing beats twenty small ornaments.
- Put the action verb near the front of the prompt: opens, cuts, seals, summons, steps through, holds back, reveals.
- Mention what is oversized or impossible explicitly.
